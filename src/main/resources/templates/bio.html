After an intense 4 months of rigorous coursework and challenging coding projects, this group was formed from individuals who persevered to the end. While many others were lost along the way, each of us was placed into a combined Code Jam due to the low numbers of our original cohort. Meeting for the first time, we each placed ourselves in an open frame of mind. Not knowing what to expect, we were all willing to devote as much time as needed to deliver an ideal product.

Met with strenuous obstacles of learning another software system as well as planning out the different coding objectives, full-time employment with Amazon, and learning one another, we each brought our abilities to the table to produce “The Music Box.” “The Music Box” was a brilliant idea presented by our Product Manager, Tristan Eason. Tristan presented his idea to us along with his color schemes for the design, and instantly the team saw his vision and produced the brilliant work you are seeing here today. Through adversity, we all made it to the finish line and would like to welcome you to enjoy… The Music Box.
